About us

Madness records is a record label founded by Tuff Genius which aims to provide a voice for underground artists who are not pushed by mainstream media. We are a full-service organization, supporting our diverse roster of talent via artist management. Tuff Genius has been one of hip-hop's most influential producer and has been present in the scene since the late 80's although his apparence remains a mystery to all.

Our Artists

ZADO

Zado is St. Louis' newest upcoming artist following in the footsteps of his peers and aims to truly put Missouri on the hip-hop map

NATE JOËL

Nate Joël started rapping early and his love for it only increases as time goes on. His earliest inspiration came from listening to rappers such as Nas, Lupe Fiasco, Rakim and many more as he aimed to emulate their techniques and learn what made them special. His songs can only be described as “Hip Hop with a soulful bounce”.

BEN BEAL

Ben Beal is a 21 year old lyricist from the suburbs of New York. He is a versatile hip hop artist who showcases his talent on any style of production ranging from mellow Lo-Fi beats to 808 influenced trap. Not only has he collaborated on songs with names such as Sophie meiers and Atlas, but he continues to work with some of the most unique producers around the globe including SwuM, FloFilz, Wun Two, Biskwiq and Beatboxbandit.
